## TEMPLATE B: Backdate / Make Whole Appeal Letter

```
TO: Ontario Disability Support Program (ODSP)
Caseworker: Sarah
ODSP File: [File Number]

FROM: Matthew Herbert
Email: mattherbert01@gmail.com
DATE: [Today's Date]

RE: Request for Retroactive Benefits — Effective July 2025
    ODSP Directive 2.1 — Date of Eligibility

Dear Sarah,

I am formally requesting a retroactive adjustment of my ODSP benefits to
July 2025, consistent with my date of initial eligibility under the Ontario
Disability Support Program Act (1997, S.O. 1997, c. 25, Sched. B, s. 4(1)).

BASIS FOR RETROACTIVE DATE:

My December 3, 2025 approval acknowledged my continuous and substantially
limiting disability, including:
  • Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) — onset 2001, age 12
  • Crohn's Disease — diagnosed 2002
  • Permanent ileostomy — since 2015
  • 43-day acute hospitalization at Cambridge Memorial Hospital
    (June 26–August 8, 2025): 29% body weight loss under hospital supervision

My condition fully met the ODSP eligibility standard (s. 4(1): substantial
physical and mental impairment, continuous, expected to last one year or more)
as of July 2025.

ADMINISTRATIVE BARRIERS (Duty to Accommodate):

The delay between my July 2025 application and December 2025 approval was not
due to a lack of eligibility. It was caused by documented administrative barriers:
  • 32 call attempts across 22 occasions that failed due to phone instability
  • TBI-related cognitive processing barriers (inability to navigate complex
    multi-step phone systems)
  • Medically incapacitated during 43-day CMH admission (June 26–Aug 8, 2025)

These barriers constitute a failure of the Duty to Accommodate under the
Ontario Human Rights Code, and per ODSP Directive 2.1, the effective date
of eligibility should reflect the date of application/eligibility onset
(July 2025), not the date of approval (December 2025).

REQUEST:
I respectfully request that:
1. Benefits be calculated retroactively from July 2025
2. All applicable benefit amounts for July–November 2025 be paid
3. Out-of-pocket ostomy supply costs ($800–$1,100/month) be reviewed
   for the retroactive period

ACCOMMODATION NOTE:
Due to TBI, all correspondence must be in writing. Written confirmation of
receipt of this letter and a written timeline for review are requested.

Respectfully,

Matthew Herbert
ODSP File: [File Number]
mattherbert01@gmail.com
[Address]
[Date]
```

## NOTES FOR ODSP CORRESPONDENCE:
- Always reference your ODSP file number on every letter
- Send by registered mail OR deliver in person with stamped receipt copy
- Keep copies of everything — date and method of send
- Follow up in writing if no response within 10 business days
- If escalating: ask for supervisor in writing, then appeal to Social Benefits Tribunal
