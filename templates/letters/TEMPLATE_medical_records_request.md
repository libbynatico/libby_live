## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:
1. Fill in: Hospital name, address, DOB, OHIP, date range, specific records needed
2. Check the appropriate boxes
3. Send via: registered mail OR deliver in person and request a stamped copy
4. Track the 30-day deadline from confirmed receipt
5. If no response: escalate to IPC (Information and Privacy Commissioner) or PHIPA complaint

## IF DENIED:
- Request written reason for denial
- Appeal to: Information and Privacy Commissioner of Ontario
